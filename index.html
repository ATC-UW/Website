Be a part of something great

Since its inception in 2019, the Algorithmic Trading Club, formerly the Financial Engineering Club, has passionately guided students through the exhilarating world of quantitative finance. Our commitment is to empower members with hands-on expertise, covering foundational Python programming to advanced data science methodologies. Engaging workshops delve into backtesting and deploying trading algorithms, shaping a robust foundation for future careers. Beyond workshops, our vibrant community participates in captivating projects and learns from distinguished guest speakers. Thriving on challenges, we host competitions that showcase talents. As we organize large industry events, members gain exposure to cutting-edge trends.
